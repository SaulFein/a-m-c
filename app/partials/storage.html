Storage

Authentic Motorcars can store your collector car or truck. We have secure access, heated buildings with security cameras, alarm, and fire sprinklers. Your car will be covered if desired and put on a battery tender periodically and we also have the ability to offer a concierge service including washing, detailing, and regular servicing at an extra cost. With our service technician on staff you are assured in having us available when your car has a mechanical need. Rates start at $350 per month, please inquire on our availability.
